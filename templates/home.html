Who are we?

what we are?

Temaribet is a tutor-providing company, with a vision of helping students discover the pleasure of learning. We recruit, train, and place competent campus students as a tutor.

Our beliefs and values

We believe students will come to experience the pleasure of learning if they are tutored in an engaging, interesting, and natural way. And once they have experienced that pleasure of learning they will be a lifelong.

Our vision

Is to create a generation of high-achieving students, who love learning, know the pleasure of it.
